If you do NOT pay the full balance:

Amortization

Amortization = paying a loan through fixed monthly payments.

Each payment has two parts:

Principal Portion

Reduces your actual debt

Interest Portion

Payment to the bank for lending you money

Amortization Pattern:

In Early Months:

  • Interest portion is higher
  • Principal portion is smaller
  • Most payment goes to interest

In Later Months:

  • Principal portion becomes higher
  • Interest portion becomes smaller
  • More payment reduces actual debt

Understanding Amortization:

This is why paying extra in the early months can significantly reduce your total interest paid over the loan term!
